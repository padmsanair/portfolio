Who I Am


A visionary Educational Leader with over 25 years of experience shaping academic excellence and institutional growth. Currently serving as Principal at Spring Dale Public School, Firozabad, I specialise in strategic curriculum development, faculty empowerment, and operational management.

My leadership philosophy centres on cultivating value-based, student-first environments — bridging rich traditional educational values with progressive modern policies to build robust, future-ready school systems.
